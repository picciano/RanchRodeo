# Ranch Rodeo

Ranch Rodeo is an iPad and iPhone app for organizing a ranch rodeo event. It takes the headache out of building fair, well-balanced teams from your roster of riders — so you can spend less time juggling names on paper and more time at the arena.

## What it does

**Keep your roster in one place.** Add every rider who's signing up, mark whether they're a child, parent, roper, or new rider, note whether their waiver is signed, and pick how many rides they're requesting.

**Generate teams automatically.** Tap *Generate* and the app builds teams of four that follow the rules ranch rodeos run on:

- No two children, ropers, or new riders on the same team
- Riders never ride back-to-back team numbers — a comfortable wait between rides is built in
- Children are always paired with one of their parents on each ride
- Riders who asked for extra rides get them, spread across the schedule

**Adjust teams by hand when you need to.** Move riders between teams, remove someone who scratched, or shuffle the lineup right up until the event starts. The app flags any team that's short a rider or has an unsigned waiver so nothing slips through.

**Save, share, and reload rosters.** Export your roster as a file you can email, AirDrop, or save in Files — and load it back next year. Rosters can also be exported as a spreadsheet for editing in Excel or Numbers.

**Print clean team sheets.** A built-in print preview lays out every team on tidy pages that you can AirPrint, save as PDF, or share — ready to hand to scorers, announcers, and pen wranglers.

## Who it's for

Ranch managers, jackpot organizers, 4-H leaders, and anyone running a ranch rodeo who'd rather hit "Generate" than spend an evening at the kitchen table with a notepad.
