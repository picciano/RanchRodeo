# Ranch Rodeo

Ranch Rodeo is an iPad and iPhone app for organizing a ranch rodeo event. It takes the headache out of building well-balanced teams from your roster of riders — so you can spend less time juggling names on paper and more time at the arena.

## What it does

**Keep your roster in one place.** Add every rider who's signing up, note whether their waiver is signed, and pick how many rides they're requesting.

**Generate teams automatically.** Tap *Generate* and the app builds teams of four with a comfortable wait between each rider's rides, so nobody is asked to ride back-to-back.

**Adjust teams by hand when you need to.** Move riders between teams, remove someone who scratched, or shuffle the lineup right up until the event starts. The app flags any team that's short a rider or has an unsigned waiver so nothing slips through.

**Look up a rider's schedule.** The Schedule tab shows each rider with the team numbers they're on — perfect for answering "what teams am I on?" without flipping through team sheets.

**Save, share, and reload rosters.** Export your roster as a file you can email, AirDrop, or save in Files — and load it back next year. Rosters can also be exported as a spreadsheet for editing in Excel or Numbers.

**Print clean sheets.** Print previews for both teams and the rider schedule lay things out on tidy pages that you can AirPrint, save as PDF, or share — ready to hand to scorers, announcers, and pen wranglers.

**Works offline.** Every feature runs entirely on your device. No internet, cell service, or Wi-Fi required at the arena.

## Who it's for

Ranch managers, jackpot organizers, 4-H leaders, and anyone running a ranch rodeo who'd rather hit "Generate" than spend an evening at the kitchen table with a notepad.
