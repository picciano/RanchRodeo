Privacy Policy

Effective: June 11, 2026

Support Privacy Terms

Summary

Ranch Rodeo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you enter into the app stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

Information the app does not collect

  • No accounts, sign-ins, or user profiles.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
  • No advertising identifiers or third-party advertising networks.
  • No crash reporting that includes identifying information.
  • No location data.

Information you enter

You may choose to enter rider names and rider details (such as whether a waiver has been signed or the number of rides requested) so the app can build teams for you. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. The app does not transmit this information off your device.

iCloud and device backups

If you have iCloud Backup enabled in your device's system settings, Apple may include the app's local data in your encrypted device backup. This is handled entirely by iOS and is not something Ranch Rodeo controls or has access to.

Sharing and exporting

When you use the Save Roster, Save as Spreadsheet, or Print features, you choose where the resulting file goes — for example email, AirDrop, the Files app, or another app you've installed. The Ranch Rodeo app does not see or transmit anything outside of the destination you select.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Any rider information that ends up in the app is entered by the adult organizer using the app, not by riders themselves.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will appear on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@elkechoranch.com.
